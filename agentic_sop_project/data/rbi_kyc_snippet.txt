Title: KYC Compliance SOP

Objective: To ensure compliance with RBI-mandated Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements for all financial transactions.

Scope: Applies to all new customer onboarding and periodic KYC updates.

Procedure:
1. Collect valid Proof of Identity (Aadhar, Passport) and Proof of Address during onboarding.
2. Verify PAN card details through the official verification portal.
3. Conduct periodic KYC reviews for existing customers as per RBI guidelines.
4. Maintain secure electronic records of all KYC documentation.
5. Report any suspicious activity to the designated compliance officer.

Compliance: All KYC procedures must strictly adhere to the latest RBI circulars and guidelines.
