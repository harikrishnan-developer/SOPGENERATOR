Title: Employee Expense Reimbursement SOP

Objective: To define the process and guidelines for reimbursement of employee-incurred expenses.

Scope: Applies to all full-time employees requesting reimbursements for travel, meals, accommodation, or other approved business expenses.

Procedure:
1. Submit scanned receipts within 7 days of expense.
2. Fill the reimbursement form via internal portal.
3. Manager approves; Finance processes payment within 5 working days.

Compliance: Only expenses supported by valid receipts will be reimbursed. Policy reviewed annually.
