Title: Finance Department Overview SOP

Objective: To define the roles, responsibilities, and key processes managed by the Finance Department.

Scope: Applies to all employees interacting with the Finance team across business units.

Procedure:
1. The Finance Department is responsible for managing financial records, budgeting, vendor payments, employee reimbursements, statutory compliance, and financial reporting.
2. All requests for financial transactions must be submitted through the official Finance Request Portal.
3. Regular reports are submitted to executive management on a monthly basis.

Compliance: All financial transactions must adhere to internal policies and regulatory requirements.
