Title: Vendor Invoice Processing SOP

Objective: To ensure timely and accurate processing of vendor invoices.

Scope: All vendor-related invoices across departments.

Procedure:
1. Vendor submits invoice to accounts@company.com.
2. AP clerk verifies purchase order (PO) match.
3. Approvals routed through procurement.
4. Payment processed within 15 business days.

Compliance: All invoices must match a valid PO and be approved prior to payment.
