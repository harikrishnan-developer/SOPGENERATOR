Title: GST Compliance SOP

Objective: To ensure accurate and timely compliance with Goods and Services Tax (GST) regulations.

Scope: Applies to all financial transactions subject to GST within the company.

Procedure:
1. Monthly GST returns must be prepared and filed through the government portal by the 15th of each month.
2. Input tax credit must be reconciled with vendor invoices.
3. Any discrepancies must be resolved before filing the final return.
4. Records of filed GST returns must be maintained for a minimum of 6 years.

Compliance: All GST filings must adhere to current regulations as outlined by the GST Council.
